Astable Multivibrator


>1) Connect the Supply to input terminals.

2) Connect output terminals to the CRO input.

3) Press the "check" button to check whether the connections are correct or not.

4) Vary the resistance using the slider after making sure the connections are correct. Press the calculate button to determine the pulse width(τP).

5) Observe the capacitor and output characteristics for each value of pulse width.

6) Press "Add to table" to add the quantities to the table.

7) Repeat steps 4-6 for different values of resistance.

8) Press the "reset" button whenever you want to refresh your simulator.
